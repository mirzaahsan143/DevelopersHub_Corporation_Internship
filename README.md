## 📌 Repository Overview
This repository contains five comprehensive data science tasks completed during my internship at <b>DevelopersHub Corporation</b>. Each project follows the full data science lifecycle: **Data Loading → Cleaning → Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) → Model Training → Evaluation**.

---

## 🛠️ Internship Tasks & Findings

### ✅ Task 1: Iris Flower Classification & Visualization
* **Problem:** Understand structural patterns in biological data to distinguish between species.
* **Methodology:** Utilized `Seaborn` for deep visual exploration, including correlation heatmaps and pairwise scatter plots.
* **Key Findings:** * Petal length and width are highly correlated ($r = 0.96$).
    * *Iris Setosa* is linearly separable from other species based solely on petal measurements.
* **Skills:** EDA, Data Visualization, Pattern Recognition.

### ✅ Task 2: Credit Risk Prediction (Loan Default)
* **Problem:** Predict whether a bank applicant will default on a loan to minimize financial risk.
* **Methodology:** Handled missing data via mode/median imputation. Developed classification models using **Logistic Regression** and **Decision Trees**.
* **Key Findings:** * **Credit History** is the single most important predictor of loan approval.
    * Graduates showed a significantly higher approval rate compared to non-graduates.
* **Skills:** Binary Classification, Data Cleaning, Model Evaluation (Confusion Matrix).

### ✅ Task 3: Customer Churn Analysis
* **Problem:** Identify bank customers likely to exit the service to improve retention strategies.
* **Methodology:** Applied **Label Encoding** for gender and **One-Hot Encoding** for geography. Trained a **Random Forest Classifier** to handle non-linear relationships.
* **Key Findings:** * Older, inactive members with high balances in Germany are at the highest risk of churning.
    * Achieved ~86% accuracy in predicting customer exits.
* **Skills:** Feature Importance, Categorical Encoding, Ensemble Learning.

### ✅ Task 4: Medical Insurance Cost Prediction
* **Problem:** Forecast medical insurance charges based on individual health and demographic factors.
* **Methodology:** Implemented **Linear Regression** and **Random Forest Regressor**. Compared performance using MAE and $R^2$ scores.
* **Key Findings:** * **Smoking status** is the primary driver of cost; smokers pay approximately 3.5x more than non-smokers.
    * The combination of high BMI and smoking leads to exponentially higher insurance premiums.
* **Skills:** Regression Modeling, Predictive Analytics, Statistical Correlation.

### ✅ Task 5: Personal Loan Acceptance Prediction
* **Problem:** Predict the success of telemarketing campaigns for bank personal loans.
* **Methodology:** Analyzed the UCI Bank Marketing dataset. Used **Logistic Regression** and **Decision Trees** with a focus on ROC-AUC curves.
* **Key Findings:** * **Call duration** is the strongest signal for conversion; longer conversations correlate with acceptance.
    * Students and retired individuals represent the most receptive market segments.
* **Skills:** Marketing Analytics, Business Insight Extraction, Model Optimization.
